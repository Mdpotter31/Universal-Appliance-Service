ABOUT US

At Universal Appliance, we understand that a broken appliance can be a major inconvenience, which is why we offer a team of experienced technicians trained to diagnose and repair a wide range of issues, and we use only high-quality parts from authorized vendors to ensure that your appliance works like new again. In addition to our repair services, we also sell parts for all makes and models. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or just need a replacement part, our parts department can help you find what you need. We sell on eBay as well, so you can shop from the comfort of your own home. All of our technicians are background-checked and drug-screened, and they arrive in company vehicles, so you can feel confident that you're working with professionals who take their job seriously. We service Louisville, Southern Indiana, up to Lagrange, KY, and Irvington, KY, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule and meet your needs. Here, we pride ourselves on providing the best possible service to our customers. Contact us today to schedule an appointment and experience the Universal Appliance difference.
